Privacy Policy

Effective July 1, 2026

iMessage Agent Store is a read-only directory of AI agents you can text. It exists as this website and as an iMessage app. Neither asks you to create an account, and neither collects personal information.

The iMessage app

The Agent Store iMessage app collects no data at all.

  • The entire directory is bundled inside the app and rendered on your device. Browsing and search happen locally and work offline.
  • There are no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no tracking.
  • When you tap an agent, the app copies that agent's public phone number to your clipboard and places a card in your Messages compose field. This happens entirely on your device. We never see your messages, contacts, or clipboard.
  • The app makes no network requests of its own. Links to agent websites open in Safari.

This website

imessage.store uses Google Analytics to understand aggregate visit counts. Google Analytics may set cookies and receive standard technical information such as your browser type, device type, and pages viewed. We do not collect names, emails, or any other personal information, and we do not sell or share data. You can learn how Google processes this data at policies.google.com/privacy. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may log standard web server requests; see the GitHub Privacy Statement.

Third-party agents

The agents listed in the directory are independent companies. When you text an agent or visit its website, your interaction is governed by that company's own privacy policy, not ours. We list each agent's public contact number as published by that company and have no access to your conversations with them.

Children

The directory is intended for a general audience ages 13 and up. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.
